## SERVICE CATALOGUE

### SERVICE 1 — RESTAURANT SETUP & CONCEPT FEASIBILITY

**Who needs this:** First-time restaurant owners, investors entering the F&B space, entrepreneurs converting a property.

**What you deliver:**
- Full concept viability study (location, demographic fit, competition mapping)
- Startup cost modelling — fit-out, equipment, licensing, working capital
- Break-even and payback period analysis
- Menu concept costing and recommended price band
- Licensing and compliance roadmap (food safety, liquor, fire, planning)
- Entity structure advice (sole trader vs. Ltd, VAT registration timing)
- Opening week cash flow forecast
- Staff structure and payroll cost model

**Technology included:** Flivio onboarding + Restaurant-IQ Bot setup from Day 1

**Deliverable:** Feasibility Report + Financial Model (Excel) + 90-Day Launch Plan

**Positioning line:** *"Know your numbers before you sign the lease."*

**Who needs this:** Any operating restaurant where margins feel tight, food costs are creeping up, or the menu hasn't been reviewed in over 12 months.

**What you deliver:**
- Full menu costing — every dish, every ingredient, every portion
- Identification of Stars, Plowhorses, Puzzles, and Dogs (menu matrix analysis)
- GP% target setting by category (starters, mains, desserts, drinks)
- Supplier cost benchmarking and renegotiation brief
- Portion control and waste reduction protocols
- Seasonal repricing recommendations
- Psychological menu pricing strategy
- Recipe standardisation to lock in margins

**Technology included:** Flivio for live food cost tracking; Restaurant-IQ for ongoing invoice capture and cost drift alerts

**Deliverable:** Menu Optimisation Report + Repriced Menu + Cost Card Library

**Positioning line:** *"You don't have a revenue problem. You have a margin problem. Let's fix it."*

**Who needs this:** Owners who only see their numbers at year-end, or who are making decisions without reliable monthly figures.

- 12-month trailing performance analysis

  - Occupancy cost % target: 8–12%
  - EBITDA margin target: 10–18%
- Monthly management accounts template and ongoing review cadence
- Bank reconciliation and cash flow visibility tool
- VAT health check

**Technology included:** Restaurant-IQ weekly reports feed directly into management account format; Flivio provides real-time cost-of-goods data

**Deliverable:** Financial Health Report + Management Accounts Template + Monthly KPI Dashboard

**Positioning line:** *"Your accountant files your tax return. I tell you why your business is bleeding."*
