About The Firm

Food Economics Applied to Real Business Decisions

The Food Economist Group is a specialist advisory practice operating across the food value chain — from farm gate to restaurant plate. Our work spans food manufacturing, branded goods, restaurant operations, hospitality investment, and food policy.

What distinguishes our practice is the combination of formal food economics training, professional financial qualifications, and three decades of hands-on experience inside food businesses. We understand the margins, the seasonality, the supplier dynamics, and the operational realities that general business advisers do not.

"I am a Chartered Accountant and Food Economist who has worked inside the full lifecycle of food businesses — from concept and licensing through to operations, pricing, scale, and exit. Every engagement I take is backed by proprietary technology so my advice is always grounded in your real numbers, not guesswork."

EPR & Packaging Compliance Division

For Food & Drink Brand Owners

Extended Producer Responsibility (EPR) legislation creates new statutory obligations for food and drink producers. Non-compliance carries significant financial penalties. Our compliance division provides precise liability forecasting and full regulatory support.

  • EPR scheme registration and scheme selection
  • Statutory liability calculation and forecasting
  • Packaging data collection, classification, and reporting
  • Compliance audit and gap analysis
  • Plastic Packaging Tax advisory and filing support
  • DEFRA regulation updates and ongoing monitoring
