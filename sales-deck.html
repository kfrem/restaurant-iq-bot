Cost Engineering

Why £49.99 works — and how we keep AI costs below £8

TradeFlow uses a tiered AI model system that automatically routes to the most cost-effective provider for your usage level. You keep the margin.

1

0–49 clients: Google Gemini Flash (Free)

1.5 million tokens/month free. At typical usage (5,000 tokens/client/month), this covers 300 clients at zero AI cost.

2

50–99 clients: Groq / Llama (Free)

Groq's free tier handles fast inference at scale. Zero cost at this tier as well — pure margin for you.

3

100+ clients: Claude Haiku (Paid — still cheap)

Claude Haiku costs ~$0.0004 per text analysis and ~$0.012 per weekly report. Even at 1,000 entries/month: under $3/client. Well inside budget.

"At 100 restaurant clients each sending 200 AI calls/month plus 4 weekly reports, total Claude Haiku cost is approximately £1.50 per client per month. The remaining £48 is margin."
